Candidates

Candidates management

Candidates can be managed by the human resources personnel, candidates can be created, retrieve, updated and deleted as well as convened to pass the evaluation test.

Questions

Questions management

Questions are managed by evaluators. they can be created, retrieved, updated and deleted. quizzes are automatically generated from these questions when a candidate is convoked.

Answers

Quiz answers

When a candidate passes a quiz, each answer is evaluated appropriately in a transparent manner by the evaluators in order to give the HR personnel a clear grasp of a candidate's abilities.

Results

Quiz results

After a quiz is evaluated by an evaluator, Human resources personnel can then proceed to either accept or reject candidates based on the final score on their tests and other factors.
